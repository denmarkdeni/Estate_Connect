Services

01

Connect to Engineers

The process of establishing communication and collaboration with a qualified engineer to oversee, design, assess, or provide technical guidance on construction projects. This connection ensures that engineering principles, safety regulations, and project specifications are properly applied throughout the construction process.

02

View 2D Plan

A 2D plan view is a flat, top-down representation of a structure or space in two dimensions, typically used in architectural, engineering, and construction drawings. It provides a bird’s-eye view of the layout, showing walls, doors, windows, furniture, and other structural elements with dimensions and annotations.

03

Generate 3D Plan

The process of creating a three-dimensional digital model of a space or structure based on architectural or engineering designs. This 3D representation provides depth, perspective, and realistic visualization of the project, allowing for better understanding and analysis compared to traditional 2D drawings.
